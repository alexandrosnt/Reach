title: General Settings
description: Configure Reach to your liking.
---

Settings are accessed by clicking the gear icon in the title bar. You can also hit **Ctrl+,** (or **Cmd+,** on Mac) to open them directly.

The settings panel has 6 tabs: **General**, **Appearance**, **Security**, **Sync**, **Backup**, and **AI**.

## General

- **Language**: Pick from English, German, French, Greek, Italian, or Bulgarian.
- **Default Shell**: The shell used for local terminal tabs. Options include bash, zsh, powershell, and cmd.
- **Startup Behavior**: Toggle "Restore tabs from previous session" to reopen your last tabs on launch.
- **Minimize to Tray**: Keep the app running in the system tray when you close the window. Desktop only.
- **Start with System**: Auto-launch Reach when you log in. Desktop only.

## Appearance

- **Theme**: Dark, Light, or System (follows your OS preference).
- **Font Size**: Slider for terminal text size, from 10px to 20px.
- **Terminal Font**: Pick the monospace font used in terminals.

## Security

- **Master Password**: Set or change a password for offline vault access.
- **Lock/Unlock**: Manually lock the vault. This clears decrypted secrets from memory.

## Sync

Covered in the [Cloud Sync](/features/cloud-sync) section. This is where you configure Turso credentials, identity info, and personal sync settings.

## Backup

Covered in the [Backup](/features/backup) section. Export and import encrypted backups of your data.

## AI

Covered in the [AI Assistant](/features/ai-assistant) section. Enable or disable the AI assistant, set your API key, and pick a model.
